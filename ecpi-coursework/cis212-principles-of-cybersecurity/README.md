# Principles of Cybersecurity (CIS212)

## Course Overview
In this course, I gained a profound understanding of the fundamental concepts of cybersecurity, encompassing the general security principles essential for maintaining a secure computing environment. I explored a diverse array of security methodologies, alongside the technologies and concepts critical for implementing robust security measures. Upon successful completion, I was adept at examining and articulating general cybersecurity fundamentals and their practical implementation techniques, preparing me to address contemporary cyber threats.

## Key Learning Outcomes
- **Threat and Vulnerability Investigation:** Conducted in-depth investigations into various cybersecurity threats and vulnerabilities, developing a keen eye for potential weaknesses.
- **Cybersecurity Implementations:** Explored and analyzed cybersecurity implementations across a variety of environments, understanding adaptive security strategies.
- **Security Operations and Incident Response:** Gained comprehensive knowledge of security operations and incident response frameworks, preparing me to effectively manage and mitigate security incidents.
